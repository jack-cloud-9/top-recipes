Pan Seared Steak

Here is a recipe for a Pan Seared steak. This recipe was generated by VSC Copilot.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds beef top sirloin steak
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Place steak in a shallow glass dish. In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, soy sauce, vinegar, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, garlic, and pepper. Pour over steak. Cover, and marinate in the refrigerator for 6 hours.
  2. Preheat grill for high heat.
  3. Lightly oil the grill grate. Grill steak 7 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.
