Italian Chicken

Here is a recipe for Italian Chicken. This recipe was generated by GitHub Copilot.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix the olive oil, garlic, basil, oregano, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place chicken breasts in the baking dish. Coat with the olive oil mixture.
  4. Bake 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.
  5. Remove the baking dish from the oven. Spoon marinara sauce over each chicken breast, then sprinkle with Parmesan and mozzarella cheese.
  6. Return to the oven and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
